Program Arduino to reset toner cartridges, and refill laser toner

Step 1: Purchase refill toner. Can be bought anywhere Replacement Toner
Step 2: Wrap the toner well with paper towel and tape down. Prep the cartridge
Step 3: Use drill and unscrew side of cartridge. Remove side panel
Step 4: Shake excess toner out, and then take the toner cap off. Shake and roll!
Step 5: Identify the proper color for the cartridge, and get ready to fill. Prepare to fill cartridge
Step 6: Gently shake the toner in. Shake carefully!
Step 7: Ensure rods are in aligned in the hole, and reattach cartridge side. Don't get frustrated here. Take your time.
Step 8: Ensure rods are in aligned in the hole, and reattach cartridge side. Again, be patient here.
Step 9: Go back to Step 2 and repeat the process for remaining cartridges.
Step 10: Get your Arduino ready! Check out DIY Tinker's video and come back after you've downloaded his code link! Hack Ricoh Aficio
Step 11: After downloading the code, fire up Arduino Studio and follow this excellent guide on uploading your code (called a sketch) to the Arduino)Get your Arduino ready! Uploading a sketch
Step 12: Now, let's go back and watch how to reset the counter on those cartridges Repeat as needed. You're all set! You just saved a lot of money!
